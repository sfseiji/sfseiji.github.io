Programs & Collaborations

Our group has also been actively working on a large variety of international large programs with friendly collaborators across the world.

JWST Projects

VENUS (Cycle4-5)

PI: S. Fujimoto, D. Coe

Large program of JWST's first wide lensing cluster survey to explore the earliest galaxies, AGNs, stars and their explosions.

Learn more
MINERVA (Cycle4)

PIs: A. Muzzin, D. Marchesini, K. Suess

Large program to perform monogeneous NIRCam medium-band imaging for JWST legacy fields of CEERS, PRIMER-UDS & COSMOS, and GOODS-N.

Learn more
CAPERS (Cycle3)

PI: M. Dickinson

Large program to perform NIRSpec MSA prism spectroscopy of hundreds z>9 galaxies in legacy CANDELS fields.

Learn more
COSMOS‑3D (Cycle3)

PIs: K. Kakiichi, X. Fan, F. Wang, E. Egami, J. Lyu

Large program to perform NIRCam imaging and slitless spectroscopy over the COSMOS‑Web field to study early galaxies, AGNs, and cosmic reionization.

Learn more
POPPIES (Cycle3, Pure Parallel)

PIs: J. Kartaltepe & M. Rafelski

Wide‑field pure-parallel NIRCam slitless spectroscopy survey (~1455arcmin²) probing emission‑line galaxies in blank fields.

Learn more
MEOW (Cycle3)

PIs: G. Leung, S. Finkelstein, R. Endsley

Medium program to perform MIRI imaging survey searching for obscured AGNs in the early universe.

Learn more
GLIMPSE (Cycle2)

PIs: H. Atek & J. Chisholm

Large program to perform the deepest NIRCam survey by gaining >100hrs exposure for a sinlge lensing cluster field.

Learn more
MAGNIF (Cycle2)

PI: F. Sun

Medium program to perform NIRCam slitless spectroscopy using medium band filters towards the best-studied Hubble Frontier Field lensing clusters.

Learn more
MegaScience (Cycle2)

PI: K. Suess

Medium program to perform NIRCam imaging using all meidum-band filters for lensed ~50,000 galaxies over z~0.3–12 to study galaxy evolution statistically.

Learn more
UNCOVER (Cycle1)

PIs: I. Labbe & R. Bezanson

Treasury program to achieve an efficient, strategetic NIRCam + NIRSpec survey towards the best studied lensing cluster field, Abell 2744.

Learn more
NGDEEP (Cycle1)

PIs: S. Finkelstein, C. Papovich, N. Pirzkal

Large program to perform the deepest blank-field NIRCam survey, coordinated with NIRISS slit-less spectroscopy.

Learn more
COSMOS‑Web (Cycle1)

PIs: J. Kartaltepe & C. Casey

Large program to perform the large-area (~0.5deg²) homogeneous NIRCam mapping to unveil the early structure formation in the COSMOS field.

Learn more
FRESCO (Cycle 1)

PI: P. Oesch

Treasury program to perform slitless NIRCam spectroscopic survey towards the GOODS-N and GOOD-S fields.

Learn more
CANUCS (GTO)

PI: C. Willott

JWST GTO survey using cluster lensing to study faint galaxies and early galaxy evolution.

Learn more
CEERS (ERS)

PI: S. Finkelstein

Early Release Science survey using NIRCam, MIRI, NIRSpec to chart galaxy formation and evolution in the early Universe.

Learn more
DJA (Archival survey)

PI: G. Brammer

Releasing public archive resources to the community via automated processing of JWST imaging and spectroscopic data taken in major extragalactic surveys.

Learn more
