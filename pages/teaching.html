Undergraduate course (2026–2027)

  • AST121H (AST121H1: Origin and Evolution of the Universe)
    This course explores the origin of the Universe and its contents, including chemical elements, stars, galaxies, and life. It is intended for undergraduate students in science and engineering programs, with an enrollment of up to 150 students.
