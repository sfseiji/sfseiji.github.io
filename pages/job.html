Job Opportunities

If you are interested in joining our research group, please consider the following opportunities. For further details or inquiries, feel free to contact me.

Email

Postdoctoral Positions

Postdoc opportunities will be sometime announced at the AAS job register. Availability depends on the funding situation.
Please feel free to contact me if you are interested or have any questions.

Prize Fellowships

There are several annual prize fellowship opportunities in Toronto for which I can serve as host or co-host:

  • Dunlap Fellowship
  • Arts & Science Fellowship
  • Canada Postdoctoral Research Award programs
  • DSI Fellowship
  • Schmidt AI Fellowship
  • CITA Fellowship

Please reach out to me if you are interested in any of these fellowships for further discussion.

PhD Students

Aspiring PhD students should apply to the Department of Astronomy at the University of Toronto. After admission, you will participate in a first-year project (AST1501 or AST1500).
If our research interests align, you are very welcome to join my research group.

Undergraduate Students

There is no guarantee of availability every year, but depending on circumstances, occasional opportunities may arise.
At the University of Toronto, undergraduates may gain research experience through programs such as:

  • AST425: Research Project in Astronomy & Astrophysics – a capstone research course typically taken in the 4th year.
  • Research Opportunity Program (ROP) – early research opportunities (usually for 2nd–3rd year students).

In addition, the Dunlap Observatory Summer School sometimes offers undergraduate positions beyond UofT students.
If you are interested, please feel free to reach out, but note that opportunities are limited and depend on timing and fit.

Visiting Students

I can also host visiting students and postdocs for periods ranging from a few weeks to several months or even years. If you’re interested in visiting, feel free to reach out!
