About Me

I am Seiji Fujimoto, an Assistant Professor specializing in observational astronomy. My research is driven by a passion for exploring the mysteries of the cosmos and unraveling the origins of the Universe. Through innovative observations and interdisciplinary collaborations, I strive to bring the wonders of the universe closer to our understanding.
