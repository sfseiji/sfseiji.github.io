Hi!

I am Seiji Fujimoto, an Assistant Professor. I grew up in Kyoto, Japan, and received my PhD from the University of Tokyo in 2019. After that, I worked as a postdoctoral researcher in Copenhagen and Austin, and I joined the faculty at the University of Toronto in 2025. As an observational extragalactic astronomer, I specialize in multi-wavelength observations, using e.g., ALMA and JWST to conduct comprehensive studies of dust, stellar populations, and gas in distant galaxies.
I enjoy traveling, playing sports, photography, camping, and board games.
