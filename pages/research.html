Research

Cosmic Hisotry -- My research focuses on the formation and evolution of galaxies in the early universe, with the ultimate goal of uncovering how the first stars and black holes emerged. To pursue this, my group uses four key observational probes: high-redshift galaxies, supermassive black holes, gas and dust, and stellar explosions.
